The Sanctuary FAQ

Is there Wi-Fi for work? ▼

Yes, we offer premium high-speed fiber Wi-Fi. We ask our guests to be mindful of peak hours to ensure everyone can enjoy the sanctuary.

Is Park Cafe pet friendly? ▼

Absolutely. We have a dedicated outdoor seating area with water bowls for your companions.

Do you host private events? ▼

Yes, our sanctuary is available for evening buyouts and private community gatherings. Please email us for catering details.
